How it works:
Are you curious about the food you eat and the calories involved? Do you worry that you are eating too much, or aren't eating enough, to meet your goals? How does the amount of exercise and rest you get affect your caloric intake? With our calorie counter, you can track the foods you eat and drink, the time you consumed them, and even the mood you were in. First, tell us a few details about yourself and the weight you want to lose or gain to determine your calorie goal. Next, enter the items you consumed in a day. When you want to see your progress, hit the "Check My Progress" button.
